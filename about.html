About Me

Ashley Setiawan is an Indonesian 2D animator and illustrator with a BFA in Illustration from Parsons School of Design (2023). Some of her clients include: Kate Spade, Farfetch, Universal Music Group, Atlantic Records, Wildflower Cases, Coup De Main (for Maya Hawke and Beabadoobee), Tae Park, Celine Kwan, The Big Silly Trivia Game, and many more.
